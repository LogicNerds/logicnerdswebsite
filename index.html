People first

70% of CRM projects fail and it's not because of technology itself. The gap between the data leadership needs to make the right decisions and how the job presents itself on the ground isn't addressed with validation rules and automations. Most consultants want to rearrange your CRM. We work with you to identify the real gap and face it head on.

Process before technology

The most successful sales teams are those that focus more on sound process than technology. In fact, teams that take the time to analyze their processes and workflows before making changes to their CRM or tech stack are 2x as likely to exceed expected ROI.

Only 3% of companies are working with data that meets basic quality standards. In fact, 79% of sales data doesn't even reach the CRM at all. Understanding and addressing where the CRM gets in the way delivers completion rates of over 85% which means the data your decisions are based on real insight rather than obligations.
